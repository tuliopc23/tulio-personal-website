# Capital Financial Consulting

Capital Financial Consulting is a financial consulting company based in Brazil. it focus on wealth management and personal wealth growth. I built for them a rich web platform that has a beautiful design and a intuitive user experience and consultant experience. Charts, fast dashboards, rich data visualizations, imagine a guided top notch personal financial app, but with a professional guiding and keeping track of your financial health.

Stack: React, Postgres, Hono.
