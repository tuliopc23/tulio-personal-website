# Nomadz Trips

Nomadz Trips is an international travel agency that specializes in organizing trips for people who want to explore the world. its focus is on providing unique group experiences that allow travelers to connect with each other. Here i built an app that while are still grat on SEO with SSR Pages and a beautiful blog, still has the big bang operation behind it. with custom made dashboard, full support, stripe and content management system.

Stack: Paytload CMS, React, Tailwind CSS.
