# CardManiacs

Cardmaniacs is my indie app, a Apple Platform native app, that is a 3 in 1 app. i like it to call it a “Content Consumption Platform”. Readwise Reader Like powerfull, but totally native, built with Swift and strict concurrency, mac first, optimized for ios and ipad os. and with a beautiful design, Vim like navigations on the Card Grid, and the ability to parse and extract a variety of content types, even youtube videos and podcasts.
